# Lego City Mod - Mod Portal Description

## Short Description

A lightweight expansion mod that integrates city simulation gameplay with Factorio's automation mechanics, using Lego citizens as production catalysts.

## Full Description

### Welcome to Lego City!

Transform your factory into a thriving city with the **Lego City Mod**! This mod seamlessly blends city simulation gameplay with Factorio's core automation, creating a unique experience where your citizens work alongside your machines.

### How It Works

1. **Research City Settlement** (Red Science √ó30) to unlock City Hall and Houses
2. **Build your city** - Place buildings to increase population quota
3. **Recruit citizens** - Exchange products for money, then recruit citizens
4. **Set up production** - Citizens work in Lego Furnaces to smelt materials
5. **Automate the loop** - Citizens get tired, rest in houses, then return to work
6. **Expand** - Research new techs to unlock more buildings and increase capacity

### Key Buildings

- **City Hall**: Recruits new citizens (consumes money)
- **Regular House**: Restores tired citizens to full stamina
- **Lego Furnace**: Universal smelting furnace using citizens as catalysts
- **Market**: Exchanges products for money
